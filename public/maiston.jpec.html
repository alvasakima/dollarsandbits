Maisto R/C Lamborghini Huracan Vehicle (Colors may vary) (1:24 Scale)

Maisto-Tech 1:24 Scale Lamborghini Huracn LP 610-4 - Full-function radio-controlled vehicle

With the Huracn LP 610-4, Lamborghini is taking its purist, unmistakable design language to a whole new evolutionary level. The design is bold and edgy, with beautifully sculptural forms. Maisto Tech brings excitement to 1:24 radio control with an injection-molded body, custom wheels and a powerful motor. Officially licensed by Lamborghini, this model has all the style and appeal of the full-size super car.

  • Pistol grip controller.
  • Realistic aftermarket custom rims.
  • Controller: 2 AAA Batteries Required (not included).
  • Vehicle: 2 AA Batteries (not included).
  • Age: 8+
View larger

About the manufacturer

Maisto Tech radio control vehicles have been in the market since 2008. The company was established in 1967 and began making toys in 1978. For the utmost in quality and reliability, Maisto designs and manufactures both the vehicle and the electronics in-house.

Got this thing running (finally). It wasn't working at first..
By Midnight Distortions on June 15, 2015
Verified Purchase
Upon throwing a set of batteries in the remote and RC car i couldn't get it running. I had to test between alkalines and rechargeables, but then realized the connection terminals on the remote was iffy. I had to take the remote apart and bend the aluminum brackets so the batteries have a better connection. Once i got that part fixed, the car was juiced up ready to go. One minor detail that i thought was overlooked is that while i can get it to work on carpet, many times the car gets stuck and you can see the wheels spinning trying to get some traction. My guess is that this will only work on hard carpets, not soft which is what my carpet basically is, i don't think it's too bad but the clearance under the car is really tight so if you are looking for an RC car that will work on carpet this may or may not work. I could try to add something more to the tires like tape with some bumps (sorta like chains for winter use) to see if i can get better traction but with little clearance i don't think it'll do much. I'm currently looking for an offroad RC car that takes a similar battery setup but i plan on getting a home with tile/wood floors for easier maintenance so this car would be good for it.

But anyway i love this thing, when it moves it would freak my cat out, lol.
